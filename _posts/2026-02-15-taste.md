Taste is a matter of knowledge. Someone with good taste means she has the necessary time and resources to know the field as a whole. To know what options are there. And then they can incorporate the ones they prefer, most possibly those lesser known, into their personal brands. A connaisseur just knows more. 

But a connaisseur would argue otherwise. They think it’s a talent, a virtue, endowed to them almost by birth. 

I often find myself being moved by the most mundane pieces of music ever. I loop them over and over again. They are not even the trendy popular ones - like Taylor Swift. They are the ones so ordinary that people do not even talk about them. They do not have quirks or wits. They do not tell a smart story. 

I like the honesty in them, I am influenced by the honesty in them. I tell myself, hey, if you can’t be a great writer, at least do not cheat. Cheating is not the same as lying. Cheating is the selling of smartness, the flaunting of something everybody has, but some brags. I have the tendency to brag, that is my weakness. I do not always get moved by what I write, but I am often moved by others’ blunt honesty. 

I do not claim to be a good person. I think it is a vain effort. One cannot have the capability to define one's own nature. I can only rely on some beings higher than me to make that judgement. The only thing I can do is to be. And maybe, at my death, that pop song would play.
